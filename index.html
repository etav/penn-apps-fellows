About the Fellowship

PennApps Fellows is a 10 week summer internship program which pairs brilliant students from all over the US with thriving startups in Philly.

Why?

As students, we see a lot of our peers heading to SF and NYC every summer. This is a huge shame, since there are many awesome startups here in Philly! We founded PennApps Fellows so we show the world how exceptional the tech scene is in Philly.

How?

The first step to becoming a Fellow is to submit your application to us. If you make it to the second round, we will pass on your resume to our participating companies. It is then up to them to select who they want to interview and eventually hire. Once all that is out of the way, we talk about housing, and what kind of events you will be up to this summer!
