About the Fellowship

PennApps Fellows is a 10 week summer internship program which pairs brilliant students from all over the US with thriving startups in Philly.

[needs work] As students we see a lot of our peers heading to SF and NY every summer. However, while exploring and engaging the awesome tech community in Philly we heard many cries of 'braindrain'. In order to help our new friends, we founded PennApps Fellows so we could pair the newest talent with rapidly growing startups, right in Philly.
